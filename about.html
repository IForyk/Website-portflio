About Me

Hi, I'm Jacob, also known as Fory. I specialize in 3D design and video production, blending my passion for graphic design, video creation, and entrepreneurship to deliver impactful content. My goal is to help brands create standout videos while building strong, lasting relationships with my clients.

I believe in understanding and collaborating with my clients to ensure the content I create meets their unique needs and goals. Beyond business, I educate others about 3D design through my YouTube channel and Patreon, fostering a supportive community of learners.

Outside of work, I enjoy playing soccer, exercising, meeting new people, and traveling. Whether you're a company aiming to enhance your brand or an individual interested in 3D design, feel free to reach out. Let's create something amazing together.

What We Focus On

3D Branding Solutions

At Fory Studio, we start by deeply understanding our clients' needs. Together, we brainstorm and develop a concept that perfectly aligns with your vision. From there, we create 3D visuals that not only convey your message effectively but also bring a cinematic quality to your project.

3D Design Education

We help 3D artists grow by teaching not just 3D design, but also other visual skills and strategies. We focus on helping them stand out in the market and understand how the industry works, equipping them with the knowledge to thrive.
